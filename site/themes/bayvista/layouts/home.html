COVID-19 Information

As the Gulf Coast grows, many people are finding a church home at Bay Vista, and we hope that you will worship with us soon. Whether you have attended church regularly before, or it is a brand new experience for you, we want you to feel at home.

If you are looking for a place to serve, we welcome you and know that you can find a place of service no matter what your desire.
